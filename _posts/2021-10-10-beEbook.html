Recordless

Concept

To Remember Around You

Description

Recordless automatically keep your diary.
For protecting your privacy, Recordless don’t use Internet.

Recent century, Internet provides an amount of information. However, we can't search even what we thought yesterday, and I wish I had have an app which can automatically remember around me.

How you use the diary depends on you.

  • to remember lectures
    • when is the deadline?
  • to be immersed in memories
    • quiet sunrise at the top of the mountain, the wave of crystal clear sea...
  • to organize data
    • In shopping, well... What I looking for..
  • to reflect on yourself
    • Is my presentation easy to understand?
  • to share your memories
    • This is about when I was in an award ceremony.
  • Of course, you can use as ordinary VoiceRecorder

Features

  • Background record
    • AudioRecord or MediaRecorder
  • Textize
    • Google API?
  • Cache
    • Say "caching" to record one sentence, say "load" to check the sentence.
  • Search
    • Voice pattern matching
    • Text matching
    • Search by time(e.g. 23 Jul. (2021))
